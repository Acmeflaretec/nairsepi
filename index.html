About Us

Epigenetics is an emerging field that establishes an influence on the expression of genes through the environment and behaviour of people. It serves this basic idea— how one chooses to adapt to their problem situation and create their own environment, creates a way for their latent healing ability to express itself.

People struggling with their problem situations are stuck between seeking mental health care and trying to find solutions within their own family or by themselves. It is better to have both working together to make the most of all the possibilities at their disposal.

Thus, we at Nairs Epigenetic Wellness view clients as rational adults who can create a personalized treatment plan that suits them best by choosing from the options we offer. We offer individual counseling sessions along with group therapy sessions at an affordable cost. From allopathy, homeopathy, and Ayurveda as systems of medicine to past-life regression, hypnotherapy, quantum healing, and spiritual healing, we target all spheres of psychological, spiritual, and social wellbeing of the client.

Services

Services

Counselling

Our counselors specialize in counseling approaches like Individual Counselling, Family Counselling, Couples Therapy, Transpersonal Counselling while utilizing all forms of classic therapies like Cognitive Behavioural Therapy, Rational Emotive Behaviour Therapy, Dialectical Behaviour Therapy, Acceptance and Commitment Therapy to therapies that touch spiritual aspects of the human psyche like Hypnotherapy, Mindfulness based therapy, Quantum Healing, Spiritual Healing and Past Life Regression.

Outbound Training

We also provide outbound training to get the clients involved in fun outdoor activities like travel to Ralamandal Wildlife Sanctuary, Choral Dam, Shree Mahakaleshwar Temple and many other places, as a means to find answers to your problems through stronger social support, Medical Tourism and Wilderness Therapy.

Choosing the Right Professional

Sometimes it can be challenging to choose the right professional. We make it possible for you by offering guidance on whether a counsellor, psychotherapist, clinical psychologist, psychiatrist, physician, specialists or other mental health care professionals are the most suitable for your specific situation. We’re here to make accessing that support you need easier for you.

Our Values

We firmly believe in valuing the time, money, attitudes and preferences of our clients. Providing an age and culture sensitive talk therapy and respecting your strengths and potential , lies at the heart of our therapeutic approach. Through our skills and services, we also aim to target the people holding little awareness about the existing alternatives to therapy. We apply a holistic approach, emphasizing on the Indian methods like Yoga, Mindfulness, Meditation, Ayurveda and spiritual techniques like Past life Regression and Hypnotherapy.

We hope at Nairs Epigenetic Wellness, you and your family find a well assured and suitable support system to navigate through your difficult situation.
